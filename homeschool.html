Admission & Registration

Enrollment at Sunrise Homeschool is simple and personalized. We require:

  • Student and Parent Interviews (to understand learning needs)
  • Previous school records or reports
  • Birth Certificate and Identification Documents
  • Proof of residence (for home-based learners)
  • Commitment to learning agreement signed by parents/guardians

Our Teachers

Our teaching staff are:

  • Certified by international education bodies for IGCSE and IB programs
  • Experienced in blended and online learning
  • Passionate mentors who guide learners holistically
